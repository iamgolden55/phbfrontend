# PHB - Public Health Bureau

> A comprehensive healthcare management platform providing medical services, health information, and patient care coordination.

## Overview

PHB (Public Health Bureau) is a digital healthcare platform that connects patients with healthcare providers, enables online appointment booking, secure medical record management, and provides evidence-based health information.

**Website:** https://www.phbhealth.com
**API:** https://api.phbhealth.com
**Type:** Healthcare Management Platform
**Region:** Nigeria (with plans for expansion)
**Status:** Production (Launched November 2025)

## Core Services

### 1. Patient Services
- **Online Appointments:** Book consultations with doctors, specialists, and healthcare facilities
- **Medical Records:** Secure access to personal health records with OTP verification
- **Prescription Management:** Digital prescriptions, refill requests, repeat prescriptions
- **Telemedicine:** Virtual consultations and remote monitoring
- **Health Tracking:** Monitor vital signs, medications, and health metrics

### 2. Healthcare Provider Portal
- **Professional Dashboard:** For doctors, nurses, and medical staff
- **Patient Management:** View appointments, medical histories, treatment plans
- **Digital Prescriptions:** Issue and manage prescriptions electronically
- **Schedule Management:** Manage availability and appointment slots

### 3. Hospital/Organization Management
- **Hospital Registration:** Healthcare facilities can join the platform
- **Staff Management:** Manage medical professionals and administrative staff
- **Appointment Coordination:** Centralized booking system
- **Pharmacy Integration:** Connect with registered pharmacies

### 4. Health Information Library
- **Conditions Database:** Information on medical conditions, symptoms, treatments
- **Medicines Guide:** Comprehensive medication information and interactions
- **Live Well:** Lifestyle advice, nutrition, exercise, mental health
- **Mental Health Resources:** Support, guidance, and professional connections

## Key Features

- ✅ Multi-factor authentication with OTP verification
- ✅ End-to-end encrypted medical records

## Technical Stack

- **Frontend:** React 18, TypeScript, Vite, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Django REST Framework, PostgreSQL, Redis
- **Hosting:** Vercel (Frontend), AWS Lightsail (Backend)
- **Database:** PostgreSQL 17.6 on AWS RDS
- **Storage:** AWS S3 (medical imaging, documents)
- **Authentication:** JWT tokens with OTP verification
- **Payments:** Paystack integration
- **SSL:** Let's Encrypt (HTTPS everywhere)

## Security & Privacy

- All medical data encrypted at rest and in transit
- OTP-based access control for sensitive information
- HIPAA-aligned data protection practices
- Regular security audits and updates
- Patient data sovereignty (stored in AWS US-East-1)
- No data sharing with third parties without explicit consent
