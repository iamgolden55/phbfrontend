## Overview

The PHB Professional Registry is a centralized system for registering and verifying healthcare professionals in Nigeria. Registration allows you to:

- ‚úÖ Practice legally within the PHB healthcare network
- ‚úÖ Prescribe medications to patients
- ‚úÖ Access professional tools and resources
- ‚úÖ Receive patient appointments and consultations
- ‚úÖ Build your professional profile

**Estimated Time to Complete:** 15-20 minutes
**Application Fee:** Free
**Processing Time:** 3-5 business days after document upload

## Who Can Register?

The following healthcare professionals can register:

| Professional Type | Regulatory Body | Example Registration Number |
|------------------|-----------------|----------------------------|
| **Pharmacist** | PCN (Pharmacists Council of Nigeria) | PCN/FG/24/12345 |
| **Doctor** | MDCN (Medical and Dental Council of Nigeria) | MDCN/R/12345 |
| **Nurse** | NMCN (Nursing and Midwifery Council of Nigeria) | NMCN/2024/12345 |
| **Midwife** | NMCN (Nursing and Midwifery Council of Nigeria) | NMCN/2024/12345 |
| **Dentist** | MDCN (Medical and Dental Council of Nigeria) | MDCN/D/12345 |
| **Physiotherapist** | MPBN (Medical Physiotherapy Board of Nigeria) | MPBN/24/12345 |
| **Medical Laboratory Scientist** | MLSCN (Medical Laboratory Science Council of Nigeria) | MLSCN/24/12345 |
| **Radiographer** | RRBN (Radiographers Registration Board of Nigeria) | RRBN/24/12345 |
| **Optometrist** | OPTON (Optometrists & Dispensing Opticians Board) | OPTON/24/12345 |

### Requirements

- Must be a licensed healthcare professional in Nigeria
- Current registration with your professional regulatory body
- Valid registration number from your regulatory body
- Completed undergraduate degree in your field
- Active practice location in Nigeria

## Step-by-Step Application Process

The application form has **6 steps**. You must complete all required fields (*) in each step before proceeding to the next.

### Step 1: Personal Information üë§

This step collects your basic personal details.

#### Fields:

1. **Professional Type*** (Dropdown)
   - Select your primary profession
   - Example: If you're a registered pharmacist, select "Pharmacist"
   - **Note:** This automatically determines your regulatory body

2. **First Name*** (Text)
   - Enter your first name as it appears on official documents
   - Example: `Amara`

3. **Middle Name** (Text - Optional)
   - Enter your middle name if you have one
   - Example: `Chioma`

4. **Last Name*** (Text)
   - Enter your surname/family name
   - Example: `Okafor`

5. **Email*** (Email)
   - Provide a valid email address
   - This will be your login username
   - Example: `amara.okafor@example.com`

6. **Phone Number*** (Phone)
   - Nigerian phone number recommended
   - Include country code
   - Example: `+234 801 234 5678` or `08012345678`

7. **Date of Birth*** (Date)
   - Select from calendar
   - Must be a date in the past

8. **Gender*** (Dropdown)
   - Options: Male, Female, Other

9. **Nationality** (Text - Pre-filled)
   - Default: Nigerian
   - Change if different

**Tips:**
- ‚úì Use your legal name (as on certificates)
- ‚úì Double-check your email - you'll use it to login
- ‚úì Ensure phone number is active for verification

2. **City*** (Text)

### Step 3: Regulatory Information üìã

This is the **most important step** - it verifies your professional license.

#### What You'll See:

The system automatically detects your regulatory body based on your selected professional type:

- **Pharmacist** ‚Üí PCN (Pharmacists Council of Nigeria)
- **Doctor** ‚Üí MDCN (Medical and Dental Council of Nigeria)
- **Nurse/Midwife** ‚Üí NMCN (Nursing and Midwifery Council of Nigeria)

#### Fields:

   - This is automatically set - you don't need to enter it
   - Displays your professional regulatory body
   - Example: "PCN (Pharmacists Council of Nigeria)"

2. **Registration Number*** (Text)
   - Your official registration number from your regulatory body

     - MDCN: `MDCN/R/12345` or `MDCN/D/12345`
     - NMCN: `NMCN/2024/12345`
   - **Where to find it:** On your registration certificate
   - **Important:** Enter exactly as shown on your certificate

3. **Registration Date** (Date - Optional)
   - Date you were first registered with your regulatory body
   - Can be found on your registration certificate

4. **Registration Expiry Date** (Date - Optional)
   - If your license has an expiry date, enter it here
   - Some regulatory bodies issue lifetime registrations

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue 1: "Cannot proceed to next step"

**Symptom:** Next button doesn't work; you see validation errors in console

**Solutions:**
- ‚úì Check that all required fields (*) are filled
- ‚úì Ensure email format is correct (example@domain.com)
- ‚úì Ensure phone number is valid
- ‚úì Check that dates are not in the future
- ‚úì For Step 3, ensure registration number is entered

**Specific to Step 3 (Regulatory Information):**
- ‚úì Make sure you've entered your registration number
- ‚úì The regulatory body is auto-filled - don't worry if you can't edit it

### Issue 2: "Invalid email address"

**Symptom:** Error message saying email is invalid

**Solutions:**
- ‚úì Ensure format: `name@domain.com`
- ‚úì No spaces before or after email
- ‚úì Use a valid domain (.com, .ng, .edu, etc.)

### Issue 3: "Invalid graduation year"

**Symptom:** Error when entering graduation year

**Solutions:**
- ‚úì Year must be between 1950 and current year
- ‚úì Cannot be a future year
- ‚úì Use 4-digit format (e.g., 2015, not 15)

### Issue 4: "Regulatory body not recognized"

**Symptom:** Warning about regulatory body

**Solutions:**
- ‚úì Ensure you selected a valid professional type in Step 1
- ‚úì If you go back and change professional type, the regulatory body updates automatically
- ‚úì Your registration number should match the regulatory body format

### Issue 5: "Cannot submit - validation failed"

**Symptom:** Submit button disabled or error on final step

**Solutions:**
- ‚úì You must check the "Terms and Conditions" checkbox
- ‚úì Scroll through review page to ensure no missing information
- ‚úì Go back through steps to fill any missed required fields

### Issue 6: "Form data lost"

**Symptom:** Information disappears when navigating steps

**Solutions:**
- ‚úì Use the "Continue" and "Back" buttons - don't refresh the page
- ‚úì Don't close the browser tab until submission is complete
- ‚úì If you must leave, bookmark the page and return soon
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Currently no auto-save feature - complete in one session

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How long does registration take?**
A: The form takes 15-20 minutes to complete. After submission, review takes 3-5 business days.

**Q: Is there a registration fee?**
A: No, professional registration with PHB is currently free.

**Q: Can I save and continue later?**
A: Not currently. Please complete the entire form in one session (about 15-20 minutes).

**Q: Can I register if I'm not in Nigeria?**
A: Yes, but you must have a valid Nigerian professional registration number and must be licensed to practice in Nigeria.

### Eligibility Questions

**Q: I'm a pharmacy technician. Can I register?**
A: Currently, the registry is for fully licensed professionals (pharmacists, not technicians). Check back for updates on additional categories.

**Q: My license expired. Can I still apply?**
A: No, your license must be current and valid. Renew with your regulatory body first.

**Q: I have dual registration (e.g., nurse and midwife). What do I select?**
A: Select your primary practice area. You can note your dual registration in the additional qualifications section.

**Q: My specialization isn't in the list. What should I do?**
A: Select "Other" from the dropdown. A text field will appear where you can type your specific specialization (e.g., "Pharmaceutical Biotechnology", "Forensic Pharmacy", "Geriatric Medicine", etc.). Be as specific as possible.

**Q: Can I change my specialization after registration?**
A: Yes, you can update your specialization from your professional dashboard after approval. Major changes may require admin verification.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions recommended).

**Q: The form is in English only. Are other languages supported?**
A: Currently English only. Contact support if you need assistance.

**Q: Can I edit my application after submission?**
A: Some fields can be edited from your dashboard. Major changes require admin approval.

### Regulatory Questions

**Q: How do I find my registration number?**
A: Check your physical registration certificate from your regulatory body (PCN, MDCN, etc.). If lost, contact your regulatory body for a replacement.

**Q: My regulatory body isn't listed. What do I do?**
A: Contact PHB support. We currently support major Nigerian regulatory bodies.

**Q: Do you verify my credentials with my regulatory body?**
A: Yes, all credentials are verified with the respective regulatory bodies.

### Document Questions

**Q: What document formats are accepted?**
A: PDF, JPG, and PNG files only.

**Q: How large can my documents be?**
A: Maximum 5MB per document (2MB for passport photos).

**Q: Can I upload documents later?**
A: Yes, but your application won't be reviewed until all required documents are uploaded.

**Q: My documents are rejected. What now?**
A: Check the rejection reason in your email, correct the issue, and re-upload.
